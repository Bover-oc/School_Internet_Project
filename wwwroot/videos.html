Videos and Animations

"Crumbs" is a hand-animated short documentary about grappling with a loved one's dementia. Through an interview with the CEO of "EMDA", an organization supporting the families of Alzheimer's patients, we experience the stories of the families that face the reality of a loved one fading away. The film shows us the happy, the sad, the rough and the frustrating sides of facing Alzheimer's on a daily basis. February, 2026.
